What is barter system?

Barter system is a transaction system, wherein goods are directly exchanged without the use of money. Eg . a shoe manufacturer sells his shoes in exchange of wheat to a farmer. In such a case, both the shoeseller & the farmer have to agree to sell & buy each other’s commodities. Double coincidence of wants is essential in the barter system.
